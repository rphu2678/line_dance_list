Raymond's Line Dance List

This is my list of line dances I've learned over the years.

Memory column key (very loose definition):

  • 5: Know it well, can probably lead dance.
  • 4: Know it mostly, maybe missing a couple steps. May need a wall or 2.
  • 3: Sort of remember, but likely need to follow a couple walls.
  • 2: Faint memory. Will need to follow and success not guaranteed.
  • 1: Little to no memory
